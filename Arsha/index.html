    About Us

    WHO WE ARE


    Hoonigan Customs is your one stop shop for all your car customization needs. Located in Nairobi Kenya, the shop houses a veteran team of technicians, fabricators, designers, electricians, painters and so much more. Every day, the team works towards the goal of creating custom works of art for our clients.

    You may have seen some of our one-of-a-kind, multi-million-dollar custom car builds on our TV show or in the news, but we also specialize in smaller customizations. From wraps to wheels and everything in between, contact us today to inquire about our services.

    History & Team

    Founder and CEO of Hoonigan Customs started his now world-renowned car customization shop in 2022 with a $5000 loan from his brother, Alfred Barry. He opened a modest shop in Kisumu County , Kenya, working on mini-trucks and soon outgrew the space moving to a larger facility in the City of Nairobi, In March of 2019.


    The facility features 12 industry leading departments offering a range of extraordinary custom services for projects of any size, scope and budget. Fans from all over Kenya visit to tour Hoonigan Customs for photo opportunities with iconic vehicles and closer look behind the scenes of the legendary work being done on-site. One of Hoonigan Customs exciting local partnership is the LA Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram Dealership in Downtown Kisumu.
